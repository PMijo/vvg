FIFA 17 introduced a new single-player story campaign mode, "The Journey", for PS4, Xbox One and Windows.[10] Players assume the role of Alex Hunter (voiced and modeled by Adetomiwa Edun), a young footballer trying to make his mark in the Premier League.[11] The player is able to select any Premier League club to play for at the beginning of the season.
